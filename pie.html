Pie Shop

I created the logo as a project, forming a larger image out of simple shapes. I decided on making a company logo for a pie shop. I chose to replicate a piece of raspberry pie as a centerpiece for this design. The vibrant red colors translated well into small basic shapes and provided a stark contrast between the otherwise neutral color scheme. I also wanted to create a warm and friendly tone to the image to compliment the coloring of the dessert.
