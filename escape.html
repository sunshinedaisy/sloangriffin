The Escape

The following animated short is a story of bouncy balls who are ready to escape the life of toys and venture off on their own. The entire film was modeled and animated by myself. I started on the premise of animating two spheres, and I decided to create a story behind their journey out of the toy box. My general idea was that the box full of toy bouncy balls notices the window is left open and take it upon themselves to act. One ball decides this is its chance to escape the captivity of the toy box. After the ball makes its escape, other bouncy balls follow.
