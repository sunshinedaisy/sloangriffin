Matte Painting

This matte painting is my creation of an altered state of the Emerald City from the Wizard of Oz. I starting working off of the lower picture and added different image layers to create the topmost picture. I changed the coloring and saturation levels of six different images in order to blend them together to create the new image and a new take on the classic landscape.
