Stemd

I began working with a group of students who aspired to create a website that served as a social media platform for STEM majors. Instead of using things like Facebook or Twitter, they wanted a place where STEM related topics could be discussed without the distractions that come with other social media websites. I was called upon to create their company logo as well as designs for other marketing merchandise. I created a mobile app icon, the logo for the website itself, as well as the tee shirt, computer sticker and flyers.
