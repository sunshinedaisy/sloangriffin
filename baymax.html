When No one Is Looking

What happens when no one is looking? This is an animated short film I created putting a new spin on the saying 'dance like nobody's watching'. In the clip, I modeled my representation of Baymax from Disney's Big Hero 6 and animated him to dance after he realizes that everyone has left the room. I modeled, animated, lighted, textured and cut this animated short into a video.
