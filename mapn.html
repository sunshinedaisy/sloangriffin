Mapn

I designed for a local startup company here in Gainesville named Mapn™. Mapn™ is a video broadcasting app focused on small towns and businesses. The displayed images are mockups I made for three main pages of the company’s web application: the main map page, which acts as the center of functionality for the app, the business pin page, and the business analytics page. While designing, I strived the keep the design as simple as possible, while still maintaining an interactive experience for the user.
