Double Exposure

The following image is a double exposure piece I have created. The two major elements of the picture consist of the image of the young girl and the image of a burning building exposed in her eyes. My goal with this piece was to create the story of a young girl watching something tragic happen to her family's home. To create this mood, I desaturated the image of the girl and placed the image of the building in the area of her eyes. I also added tints of red to different areas of the image of the young girl and lightened the area of her face to emphasize the darkness of everything around her.
