GameOn-Politics

Research Focus and Context Understand the factors involved in the decision making process of people speaking about politics on social media. Furthermore, identify the problems people face when discussing politics online. User Needs 1) Users want the ability to read about political issues that interest them without seeing users fighting about the topic 2) Users want to be able to chat with only their friends and family 3) Users want notifications about major political changes in topics that they are interested in 4) Users want to filter the huddles they see based on their interests 5) Users want to see quick unbiased information about major international politics events 6) Users want to share articles or pieces of news to their huddles
