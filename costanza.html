Simple Shapes

The goal of this project was to create a graphical representation of an image using simple shapes. I decided to replicate a comical figure in pop culture, George Constanza. I brought his form to life using basic shapes and added depth using varying colors and shades. I used cooler tones to emphasize the highly recognizable Seinfeld logo in the upper left-hand corner, and I also incorporated one of his most iconic quotes to represent the true nature of his character. The font is meant to resemble that of a screenplay in order to give the impression that it is a character description.
